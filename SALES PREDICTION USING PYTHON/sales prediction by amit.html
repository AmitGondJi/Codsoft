Conclusion¶

The linear regression model shows strong predictive performance with an R² score of approximately 0.91, indicating that over 90% of the variance insales can be explained by advertising spends across TV, Radio, and Newspaper. The low Mean Squared Error confirms the model’s accuracy, making itsuitable for predicting future sales trends.¶
